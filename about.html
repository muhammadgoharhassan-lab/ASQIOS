About

An instrument for finding out — not for being right.

ASQIOS exists to combine rigorous Islamic finance governance with systematic quantitative method, in a form that can be challenged, reproduced, and disproven. The point is the discipline, not a verdict.

The mission

Discover and validate Shariah-compliant equity strategies — defensibly.

The aim is a research system that can identify systematic strategies and, just as importantly, reject the ones that do not hold up. Governance comes first, evidence is logged before it is interpreted, and every output is marked void for capital until formal validation gates are cleared.

It is explicitly pre-capital-deployment. That is not a limitation to apologise for — it is the honest stage the work is at, and the platform is designed to say so plainly.

Why "void for capital"?

Because a research finding and an investment decision are different things. Marking every output void for capital keeps the two from being quietly confused — and keeps the platform honest about what it has, and has not, earned the right to claim.

Operating principles

The convictions that shape every decision.

These are not slogans. Each one changes how the platform is built and what it is allowed to conclude.

P1

Cage before capability

The research-integrity infrastructure is built before any strategy work. The honesty spine is non-negotiable and deliberately small.

P2

Free data rejects; it never accepts

The contaminations of open data nearly all flatter long-only strategies, so a flattering result proves little — but a failure is real. The asymmetry is treated as a discipline, not a footnote.

P3

Fail closed, not open

When a component cannot decide, it returns an explicit error — never a quiet default that happens to look positive.

P4

Exclusions require hard evidence

A name is only excluded on real evidence of fraud, insolvency, or going-concern risk — never on a data anomaly, an artifact, or routine activity.

P5

A source is a channel, not a factor

The research question is which event type carries edge — never whether a data source as a whole does.

P6

Pre-register; never stop post-hoc

Minimum detectable effect and required sample are committed before any data is inspected. Post-hoc stopping rules are forbidden by the honesty spine.

The stance

Distrust acceptances. Trust rejections.

The independent review posture is the null hypothesis itself. The hardest discipline is not running the test — it is refusing to extend a verdict that the evidence has not earned, exactly at the moment it would be convenient to do so.
