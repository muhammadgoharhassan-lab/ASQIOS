What ASQIOS is

Three commitments hold the whole system together.

Compliance, method, and integrity are not features bolted onto a research process. They are the structure the research is permitted to exist inside.

01 / COMPLIANCE

Shariah governance, first in line

An AAOIFI-primary methodology with a supervisory authority holding a reserved veto over every permissibility question. No mechanism enters research without a ruling from qualified scholars.

02 / METHOD

Systematic, not discretionary

Strategies are explicit, testable rules — defined universes, cross-sectional factors, pre-registered verdict criteria — so what we believe can always be checked against what the evidence shows.

03 / INTEGRITY

An honesty spine before capability

An immutable data store, a trial ledger, and a reproduction harness sit beneath everything. Results are logged before they are interpreted, so the record is evidence — not rationalisation.

The decision order

A strict order of priorities — and it never reorders.

ASQIOS resolves every trade-off lexicographically. A lower priority is considered only once every higher one is satisfied. Efficiency can never buy its way past compliance; growth can never buy its way past survival.

[ 1 ]

Shariah complianceThe gate. A non-compliant strategy does not advance, regardless of how well it performs.

[ 2 ]

SurvivalAvoiding ruin precedes pursuing return. Continuity is protected before anything is optimised.

[ 3 ]

Geometric growthLong-run compounding — not single-period averages — is the objective once the gates above hold.

[ 4 ]

RobustnessAn edge must survive regime changes and correction for multiple testing before it is believed.

[ 5 ]

EfficiencyCost and turnover are minimised last — only after everything above is settled.
